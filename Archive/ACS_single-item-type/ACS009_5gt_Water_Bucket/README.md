Converts Iron Blocks to Water Buckets on a 5gt Clock, using Fluid Tick Manipulation to ensure the water regenerates at the correct timing even when first turned on.
## Features
- Extremely compact
- Outputs 14400 Water Buckets/hr, uses 4800 Iron Blocks/hr.
## Notes
External overflow protection is required, otherwise when storage fills up, buckets will accumulate in the dispenser, leading to empty buckets in output.
## Instructions
1. When building, to get the Hopper Minecart clipped inside the Observer, align Hopper Minecart against Fence first using a rail, then break the rail and simply push observer in from above.
2. How to use: Load up chest with Iron Blocks, then turn on lever.
## Compatibility
1.21+
