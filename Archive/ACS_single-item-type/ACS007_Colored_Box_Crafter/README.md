Crafts colored boxes at 16gt, ideal for inputs of shulker loaders where you don't want to recolor boxes later. Based off of [ACS006 Box crafter](https://discord.com/channels/856232076252282890/1480739791703244821)
## Features
- Crafts a colored box every 16gt
- Two versions: Regular Dye Input (Put crafted dye into the loader), Raw Dye Input (Put uncrafted dye into the loader (e.g., bonemeal))
## Considerations
- Breaks if it runs out of boxes
- Raw input must be 1 to 1, so no tall flowers
- Shulker unloaders cannot be activated within 4gt; there must be a difference in the log and shell and dye boxes. Follow the startup guide and always use full boxes to mitigate breakage
## Instructions
1. After the build is complete, remove the two hoppers where the shell and log boxes will go and place those boxes facing down
2. Take 8 logs out of the log box and craft them into 4 chests
3. Place the chests in the middle crafter and replace the two hoppers
4. Place the dye box and then put an item into the hopper with a comparator readout
5. Take the item out and place it again, then take it out again
